About Me

I’m Alan Van, a Computer Science student at the University of Massachusetts Lowell with a focus on Cybersecurity.

Beyond academics, I’m constantly building — whether it’s code, custom PCs, or personal projects. I’m passionate about understanding how systems work under the hood and finding practical ways to apply that knowledge.

Through hands-on experiences like co-founding Haverhill Computers and completing a range of C++ and assembly projects, I’m learning what it takes to build real things — both in software and hardware.
